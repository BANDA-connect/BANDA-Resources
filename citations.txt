BANDA stands on the shoulders of MANY:

* Refer to BANDA Data Descriptions and Technical Validations articles described above for attributions of original sources for tasks, methods, analyses, etc.

* If you use an aspect of BANDA that was indicated to have been adapted from prior research, please consider also crediting those original works.
	
	- E.g., BANDA's Incentive Processing Task was based upon Delgado and colleagues' (2000) original task, provided from the HCP-Young Adult study (see Barch et al., 2013).
